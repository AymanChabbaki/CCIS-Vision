2. Department Missions & Services
Use these detailed missions to define the data fields for your "Activity Monitoring" module.

A. Département Appui et Promotion (Support & Promotion) 

Core Missions: Organizing training and events (B2B, fairs), collecting business opportunities, economic monitoring.

Sub-Service: Service Formation et Networking.

Develop partnerships with training bodies.

Create meeting spaces for economic operators.

Establish annual training programs based on employment evolution.

B. Département Services aux ressortissants et Veille économique (Member Services) 

Core Missions: Communication plans, databases on regional economy, documentation funds, website management.

Sub-Service: Service Information et Communication.

Reception and orientation of members (fiscal, legal, financial questions).

Creating and managing economic databases.

Publishing the business directory and newsletters.

C. Département Relations Institutionnelles (Institutional Relations) 

Core Missions: Managing relations with elected officials, public administrations, and professional organizations.

Sub-Service: Service Organes de gestion et Coordination.

Organizing General Assemblies and Bureau meetings.

Drafting meeting minutes (PVs) and reports.

D. Département Stratégie et Partenariat 

Core Missions: Elaborating CCIS strategies and overseeing partnership implementation.

E. Service Audit et Contrôle de Gestion 

Core Missions: Producing management reports and dashboards, analyzing management procedures, and conducting internal audits.

F. Service Administratif et Financier (Annexes) 

Core Missions: Accounting, financial statements, personnel management, IT system management, and purchasing/assets management.

3. Public-Facing Services (Brochure Info)
These are the specific "products" or services the CCIS offers to companies, which might need to be tracked in your system:


Le Développement: Market studies, international prospecting.


La Formation: Training adapted to company needs.


L'Animation: Local partnerships and territory arrangement projects.


L'Assistance: Technical and fiscal assistance via the "Centre de Gestion de Comptabilité Agréé" (CGC).


Règlement des différends: Mediation and arbitration via the International Center of Mediation and Arbitration of Rabat.


Ouverture Internationale: Partnerships with ASCAME and CPCCAF.
